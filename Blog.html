The Art of Choosing Your Signature Scent

Finding your signature perfume is a personal journey. Explore different fragrance families—floral, woody, oriental, and fresh—to discover what resonates with your personality. Test scents on your skin and let them develop over time before making a decision.

Understanding Perfume Notes

Perfumes are composed of top, middle, and base notes. The top notes are the initial impression, the middle notes form the heart of the fragrance, and the base notes provide lasting depth. Learn to identify these layers to appreciate your perfume fully.

Tips for Making Your Perfume Last Longer

Apply perfume to pulse points like wrists, neck, and behind ears. Moisturize your skin before spraying, and avoid rubbing the fragrance in. Store your bottles away from sunlight and heat to preserve their quality.
